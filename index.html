/ 01 — what I understand about this role

The Role

🧠

Enterprise-Wide AI Strategy

This isn't an AI feature role within one product team. Mattel wants someone who can align and manage AI product development across the whole organisation — measuring and communicating business value across multiple AI initiatives simultaneously.

💡

Use Case Ideation & Prioritisation

Collaborate with business units across Product Development and Supply Chain — running ideation sessions and hackathons to surface AI opportunities. Then prioritise them. The PM is the bridge between what AI can do and what the business actually needs.

🤝

Trusted Advisor to Stakeholders

The role explicitly frames this as being a "trusted advisor" to Product Development and Supply Chain — not a technology vendor, but a partner who understands the business domain deeply enough to recommend the right AI solutions for their specific pain points.

🛡️

Responsible AI Principles & Guardrails

Establish Responsible AI principles for Mattel — collaborating with legal, security, compliance, and industry practitioners. This requires understanding both the power and the risk of AI systems, and building governance that enables adoption without creating liability.

🚀

Champion AI Through Leadership

Lead insight gathering and champion new AI-powered business models through senior management — this person doesn't just build, they persuade. Driving alignment, securing endorsement, and building organisational confidence in AI adoption are core to the job.

🌱

Growth Mindset & Continuous Learning

Mattel lists "growth mindset" twice in the job description — not an accident. They want someone genuinely curious about AI, who stays current, embraces challenges, and brings energy to the work. The AI landscape changes fast; this role demands someone who keeps up.
